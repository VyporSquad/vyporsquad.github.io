You are required to manually verify your identity before you continue using your Internet service due to a recent hacking breaches against ISP networks. Be aware that your data is secure and the suspect is currently being dealt with. Once verified, you will be prompted to change your ISP password and then access will be restored to your account. Verification is done via the card that you use to pay for the ISP, a small amount will be deposited to confirm the card exists. This amount will be restored later.

We have also agreed to give our customers one month of free internet service at their current package as a gesture of good will, as we understand manual verification is a long, time-consuming process.
